Tattoo Justification for People Who Don’t Need One

What Is “Ink Therapy” (If You Care)?

Look, some people call it ink therapy — the idea that getting tattooed helps you deal with emotional crap. Trauma, grief, identity, whatever. For most of us, it’s not that deep. It just feels right.

Fun Facts (So You Can Sound Smart While Getting Inked):

  • Ötzi the Iceman had tattoos 5,300 years ago. You’re just following tradition.
  • Ink sits in the dermis layer of your skin — that’s why it lasts. Science.
  • Tattoos can release endorphins. It’s literally a controlled pain high.
  • Some people walk out of the shop feeling lighter. Mentally, not just in the wallet.
  • If anyone asks “why?” — just stare blankly and say “Because I f***ing wanted to.”
